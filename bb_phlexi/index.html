We are a licensed and standardized company duly registered under the laws of Ghana Company's Act 179. We position ourselves to be the most reliable and trusted domestic worker employment firm and provide you with efficient, secured, and tailor-made services, specifically designed to meet your needs.

Our team are made up of highly trained and dedicated caregivers who provide comprehensive services that ranges from personal, companionship, and non-medical. We use the latest technology to ensure that you get the assistant you need to improve the quality of your life while maintaining your independence.

Bernice Boakye-Yiadom - C. E. O.

Bernice Boakye-Yiadom holds a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Acturial Science from the University of Cape Coast. She is an executive member of the Association of Business Executive - United Kingdom. Like most people, she started as a National Service Personnel (NSP) at Fidelity Asset Management Ltd (FAMAL), now known as Fidelity Securities, Osu-Accra. She was a star performer, who increased her portfolio to the amazement of her supervisor. She delights in satisfying her clients.

She worked with the Nationwide Microfinance after leaving Fidelity Securities where she served in various capacities for five years. She was porched to another microfinance company as a managing director. She spent her school vacations in the United States where she worked mostly with the elderly, kids, and children with special abilities.

Since her passion lies in helping and satisfying others, BB Phlexi Ghana Limited hire quality professionals with vision amd matured mind. She grooms the youth with abnormal behaviors to enable them secure employment in the care-giving industry.

She is a married woman with three girls, devout christian, and former missionary.

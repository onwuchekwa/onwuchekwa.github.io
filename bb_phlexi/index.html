Bernice Boakye-Yiadom - C. E. O.

Bernice Boakye-Yiadom holds a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Acturial Science from the University of Cape Coast. She is an executive member of the Association of Business Executive - United Kingdom. Like most people, she started as a National Service Personnel (NSP) at Fidelity Asset Management Ltd (FAMAL), now known as Fidelity Securities, Osu-Accra. She was a star performer, who increased her portfolio to the amazement of her supervisor. She delights in satisfying her clients.

She worked with the Nationwide Microfinance after leaving Fidelity Securities where she served in various capacities for five years. She was porched to another microfinance company as a managing director. She spent her school vacations in the United States where she worked mostly with the elderly, kids, and children with special abilities.

Since her passion lies in helping and satisfying others, BB Phlexi Ghana Limited hire quality professionals with vision amd matured mind. She grooms the youth from less privilege homes--who cannot afford schooling--to enable them secure employment in the care-giving industry.

She is a married woman with three girls, devout christian, and former missionary.

Harriet Ayisi - Administrative Assistant

Harriet Ayisi is an undergraduate of Brigham Young University, Idaho, majoring in applied health. Her responsibility is to make sure that the company achieve its strategic goals. Her service as a former missionary and education as a graduate of BYU Pathway Worldwide has prepared her to fit into her role perfectly.

When she is not working, she sings with the glorious bountiful choir based in Accra. She is the founder of the African Child Foundation, a non-governmental organization that distribute food and clothing to homeless and needy children in our community every december.

As an African Content Producer on a fairly new subject with social media users as her target audience, Alberta Mortty brings her rich digital skills to the organization.

Wedding Reception Offers

Function Rooms

The Grand Ballroom is our main function room and boasts a spectacular 736 square metres of space. Guests can rent the full ballroom for larger celebrations, or rent half the ballroom — providing an ample 368 square metres of space — for smaller ones. The ballroom itself also features a screen and projector, allowing you to add some extra touches to wedding speeches.

The pre-function area and foyer offer capacity for up to 450 guests, allowing them to sip drinks and socialise before sitting down to the main meal. There is also lots of space in our gardens and piazza for outdoor ceremonies

Wedding Catering Services

Plan your big day with the help of our expert events team who will help you with the arrangements to make your wedding day a truly stellar occasion. Our experienced planners are on hand so you can organise your day and add those personal touches that will make the day even more memorable.

Meet with our chef to design a special menu for your day, or choose from a wide selection of buffet menus, finger foods and set menus. You can try our catering out at a special tasting by the bride and groom.
